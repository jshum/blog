--- layout: post title: places to eat in providence published: true --- 1) federall hill: far away, probs half an hour by walking but great italian food. (~$20-30 per person)
2) downtown by providence place. there's a few expensive places to eat (chris ruth steakhouse, mccormick schmick, union station brewery. ~$20-30 per person)
3) wickenden, street is really close to india point if you went to fireworks. less upscale than the other two but also acceptable food. sakura, japanese food, is good. a cool one would be Abyssinia, which is ethiopian food. (~$15)
4) wayland avenue, go east on waterman for about 20 minutes. residential area. a number of nice places there i.e Twist
