--- layout: post title: external and internal world published: true tags: [posterous] --- for the external world, you must let go of the internal one.
for the internal one, you need to let go of the external one.
if you focus too much on the external one, you run the risk of forgetting yourself
if you focus too much on the internal one, you run the risk of forgetting others
expecting but not reciprocating. something i'm very guilty of.
change it must, but will it?

and instead of worrying about who you want to become,
worry about who you are.
there will always be the socialites and the scientists who have lunch on the green walking around awkwardly
you may lie in the middle, not spectacular in either world. being normal, being forgotten into oblivion. but is it so bad?
